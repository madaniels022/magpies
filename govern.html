Magpies guides you in the adoption of best practices for managing and enhancing your G Suite after the initial deployment. Our Release Governance program defines how you assess G Suite enhancements and when they are included in your release schedule. Building this rigor in your company will help you maximize your investment in G Suite.

Why do we do this?

Cloud technology has changed how improvements are delivered. This requires an adjustment to how software releases are managed. Google rapidly adds new product features and enhancements to G Suite that you can innovate with.

We've found that many companies struggle in switching to the fast-paced timeline of new feature releases in the Cloud. This diminishes one of the key benefits gained in the modern software solution. A formal and consistent process for evaluating, scheduling and deploying enhancements ensures you fully realize the value of Cloud software.
