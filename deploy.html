Google provides clear guidance on how to setup G Suite. Our expertise is in understanding and follwing Google's methodology. Our proven plan will help you deliver a successful project with impactful business results.

Below are the four major steps Google outlines for the setup of G Suite. Included are links to the related website locations. Magpies blends these instructions with your desired results to create solutions specfic to you.

Step Name Description
1 Domain Setup The first step in setting up G Suite is verifying your web domain. This can be one of the more technical steps in the process. Configuration at your domain provider needs to be completed. Google provides guidance, found here,that assists with this step.
2 G Suite Setup The next steps for your deployment depends on the size of your company. Google has created guidance for four differnt size businesses. Those four are One Person, Small Business (2-9 users), Medium Business (10-250 users), and Enterprise (250+ users). More details on this can be found here.
3 Data Migration You likely cannot start from a clean sheet of paper. Emails, calendars, documents, and various other items will need to be moved effciently, timely and safely to G Suite. This will require precise execution to ensure business continuity. Google provides guidance and tools that assist in the migration, which can be found here.
4 User Training Adoption will determine the success and value of the project. Google assists with this transition by providing online training resources, which can be found here. Often, a more personalized approach is needed. A training program specific to your company and then delivered at a time optimal for your resources will enhance adoption.
