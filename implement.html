Magpies engagements are founded on the concept of Agile. We craft each project to be divided into short deliverable cycles, also referred to as Sprints. We provide solution demonstrations at the end of each Sprint to gather feedback and determine any needed adjustments.

Why do we do this?

Agile has radically improved how technology is delivered and shortened how long it takes to complete a project. That concept of Agile is to provide earlier views of a solution so that timely feedback is provided.

The use of Agile for deploying packaged software, such as G-Suite, is rapidly expanding. A project with this framework will be more successful because it reduces the risk of deliering a soliution differnet from the user's expectations.
