Initial Setup Services for Google G Suite

Create a G Suite Account

When subscribing to G Suite, you will chose between two accounts types, either Basic or Business. We will help you evaluate the capabilities provided in each subsciption type and quickly navigate to the right choice.

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Confirm domain ownership

Once you have subscribed to G Suite, you will be required to verify that your company owns the domain. We will make the changes needed to complete the verification and quickly move to the next setup steps.

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Add user accounts

How many user accounts will you need? Will there need to be Group emails created? We will help evaluate these questions and advise you on how to optimally setup your user acccounts for the organization.

Approximate Time: Varied

Switch email delivery

Once you have your User Accounts setup, the next step is to begin to have all new emails delivered to the G Suite account. We will prepare your staff for this switch and make the technical changses necessary

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Migrate historical emails

Preserving historical emails is as important as ensuring new email is delivered. We will work with you to determine how much historical you want to transfer and leverage delivered G Suite tools to make the move.

Approximate Time: Varied

Review G Suite with users

We will schedule a web meeting using Google Meet to present the capabilities of G Suite to your organization. During this session we will walk through managing their account and the available applications.

Approximate Time: 1 hour
