Google provides clear guidance on how to setup G Suite. Our expertise is in understanding and follwing Google's methodology. Our proven plan will help you deliver a successful project with impactful business results.

Setup Basics

Create a G Suite Account

Establishing G Suite requires you chose between one of two accounts, either Basic or Business. Our staff will evaluate your organizations and help you quickly navigate to the right option.

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Confirm domain ownership

The domain used during the account creation will require verification that your company owns it. The domain manager you use (i.e. GoDaddy) will need some technical configuation to complete this process.

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Add user accounts

We will work with you to evaluate the current user accounts to determine how those are created in G Suite. Some email addresses may be better served used Groups so that several users can review emails.

Approximate Time: Varied

Switch email delivery

Our staff will asssist you in determining the least disruptive time to switch the delivery of all new email to G Suite. This will require a technical change to on the domain manager, after which emails begin to deliver to G Suite.

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Migrate historical emails

Our team will use the G Suite delivered Migration tool to move any amount of historical emails your organization wants to preserve. The time to complete the process depends on the amount of history.

Approximate Time: Varied

Review G Suite with users

We will schedule a web meeting using Google Meet to present the capabilities of G Suite to your organization. During this session we will walk through managing their account and the available applications

Approximate Time: 1 hour

Additional Setup Services

Add additional domains

Do you have additional domains for your core business or other business entities? Our staff will add these domains to your G Suite account and configure them to be used based on your business and collaboration needs.

Approximate Time: Varied

Detailed user training

Is making a technology change challenging for some of your employees? We will develop a plan and deliver individual or group training to ensure you maximize the value and minimize the impact of moving to G Suite.

Approximate Time: Varied

Application integration

Does your organization use additional applications (sales, human resources, recruting) or services (fax, employee screening)? We can add the required configuration to integrate these with your G Suite account.

Approximate Time: Varied
